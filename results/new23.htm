FAQs

What if I have not completed the paperwork process, can my athlete still try out for the team?

Yes, there is another opportunity to turn in the paperwork on July 14, from 5-7 pm at RB High.

If one of my besties decides to come out and the July deadline has passed, what can they do?

They can complete their paperwork and turn it in no later than the end of the first week of school, Aug 19.

I like training, and being with the team, but I dont really want to race, can I do that?

No, it is great that you want to be part of the group, but it is a competitive team, we train to compete, racing is part of it.

Do we practice everyday during the season and what time?

Practice is everyday and Saturday for the Varsity and everyone that wants to come.  Practice is from 4-5:30 approx.

What about uniforms, how do we get them?

I will order them and then they can be purchased. I hope you like blue and white.
